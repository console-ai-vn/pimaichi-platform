## Risk & Mitigation Log

| Risk | Trigger | Response |
|------|---------|----------|
| Low subscriber conversion | <5 subs in Week 1 | Pivot to more aggressive teasers; offer extended $3 promo |
| Instagram shadowban | Reach drops >50% | Reduce OF linking; use link in bio only; wait 7 days |
| Negative feedback | Hate comments or unfollows | Do not engage; continue content as planned |
| Twitter ban (adult content) | Account suspended | Appeal; shift focus to Reddit |
| Content burnout | Missed posting days | Reduce cadence; use content bank buffer |
| Low budget | Exceed $500 | Cut paid tools; use free alternatives |
